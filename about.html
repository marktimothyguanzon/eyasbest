Meet the Founder

The heart and vision behind Eya’s Best Coffee.

Andreya Noreyn "Eya" Rubi Guanzon

Founder & Head Coffee Curator

Eya’s Best Coffee started as a simple dream — to create a warm, welcoming space where people can slow down and enjoy thoughtfully crafted coffee.

With a passion for quality beans, local community, and genuine hospitality, Eya built the café around the belief that coffee should feel personal, comforting, and memorable.

Every cup served reflects care, creativity, and love for the craft.

Our Journey

How Eya’s Best Coffee grew from a dream to today.

2018

The Idea

Eya’s love for coffee began at home — experimenting with beans, brews, and sharing cups with friends and family.

2020

First Pop-Up

The first pop-up coffee stall launched, serving handcrafted drinks and building a small but loyal community.

2022

Opening the Café

Eya’s Best Coffee officially opened its doors — a cozy space designed for comfort, creativity, and connection.

Today

Growing Together

Today, we continue to serve quality coffee, support local suppliers, and grow with our community — one cup at a time.
